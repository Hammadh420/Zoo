Welcome To The Central Zoo

The Central Zoo is one of the biggest zoos in the town. The land area of the zoo is 33 acres. It was established in 1980 with 10 acres, and later expanded in 2000. The zoo ensures proper living space for all animals and maintains high-level human security. Regular food supply and care are also provided to all animals.

Animals

Animals are living organisms, mostly multicellular with a diverse range of forms. They are generally motile, have a nervous system, and reproduce sexually.

Birds

Birds are warm-blooded vertebrates with feathers, wings, and beaks. They lay hard-shelled eggs and are known for their diverse and often spectacular flight abilities.

Water animals

Water animals, also known as aquatic animals, are creatures that live in or around water and depend on it for survival. They are diverse, including fish, mammals like whales.







Central Zoo is a premier wildlife sanctuary offering immersive experiences with diverse fauna. Join us for unforgettable adventures and educational programs.
