Exciting Events at Zoo Park

Join us for fun, educational, and unique experiences at Zoo Park.

Upcoming Zoo Events

World Wildlife Day

Celebrate wildlife with rare animal exhibits, live shows, and educational booths.

Safari Night

Enjoy guided night tours, animal behavior shows, and glowing jungle decor.

Animal Adoption Fair

Support conservation by adopting animals. Get photo ops & meet the keepers.

Zoo Anniversary Gala

Celebrate our birthday with fireworks, performances, and history exhibits.

Reptile Encounter

Get up close with snakes, lizards, and crocodiles in a safe environment.

Birds of Paradise Show

Watch exotic birds perform tricks and learn about their habitats and sounds.

Children's Day Festival

Games, mascots, face painting, and surprises for all kids at the zoo.

Eco Awareness Day

Join volunteers in keeping the zoo clean and green, plus workshops for kids.
