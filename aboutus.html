About Us

CENTRAL Zoo is one of the biggest zoos in the town. The land area of the zoo is 33 acres. CENTRAL Zoo was established in 1980 with 10-acre land, but it was later expanded in 2000. Proper residing space for all types of animals has been made and human security is maintained at a high rate. Food supply for animals is checked regularly.
